About Me

Bishal, born on 10th June 2002, hails from the picturesque town of Jorhat in Assam. His early years were spent amidst the vibrant culture and serene beauty of his hometown. A dedicated and focused individual, Bishal laid the foundation for his academic journey at Carmel School, Jorhat, where he completed his schooling.

For his higher secondary education, Bishal attended Kendriya Vidyalaya, Jorhat, where he honed his skills and developed a strong academic profile. His passion for technology and innovation guided him toward pursuing higher studies in Computer Science and Engineering. These formative years shaped his aspirations and deepened his understanding of his chosen field.

Currently, Bishal is pursuing his B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Integrated) at Tezpur University, Assam. Immersed in a rigorous academic environment, he is actively developing the technical skills and knowledge required to excel in the fast-evolving tech industry. His journey reflects his commitment to learning and growing as a future engineer.
